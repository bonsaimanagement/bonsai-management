# Bonsai Management

**Growing steadily, one home at a time.**

Bonsai Management is a residential property management company specializing in single-family rental homes in Columbus, Georgia. Founded in 2024, we take a hands-on, detail-oriented approach to managing a growing portfolio of investment properties.

## What We Do

Bonsai Management oversees every aspect of our rental properties — from tenant coordination and maintenance management to market-driven rental pricing and proactive property oversight. Our turnkey model combines local property management with active owner involvement, ensuring each home in our portfolio receives the attention it deserves.

### Our Portfolio

We currently manage 3 single-family rental homes in the Columbus, GA area, with plans to expand in 2026. Our properties serve military families, civilian employees, and other residents of the Columbus community.

### Our Approach

Like the bonsai tree our company is named after, we believe in patient, careful cultivation. We're not trying to scale fast or become the biggest — we're building a small portfolio deliberately, one property at a time, with meticulous attention to each home and every tenant relationship.

## About This Website

This repository contains the source code for the Bonsai Management company website — a professional online presence for our property management business. The site serves prospective tenants, current tenants, and potential partners/vendors with:

- **Property listings** with photos, amenities, and neighborhood details
- **Tenant resources** including move-in guides, maintenance procedures, and FAQs
- **Area guides** for the Columbus, GA neighborhoods we serve
- **Market analysis** covering rental trends in our target markets
- **Vendor directory** of our trusted local contractors and service providers
